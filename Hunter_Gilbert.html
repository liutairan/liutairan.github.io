Dr. Hunter Gilbert

ASSISTANT PROFESSOR OF MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

Department of Mechanical & Industrial Engineering
Louisiana State University
Baton Rouge, LA 70803, U.S.A
Email: hbgilbert@lsu.edu

Hunter Gilbert received the B.S. degree in Mechanical Engineering from Rice University, Houston, TX, in 2010. He received the Ph.D. degree in Mechanical Engineering from Vanderbilt Univeristy, Nashville, TN, in 2016. He joined the faculty at LSU as a research assistant professor in August 2016, and is currently on a fellowship from the Alexander von Humboldt foundation to conduct research at the Max Planck Institude for Intelligent Systems in Stuttgart, Germany. In August 2017, he joins the faculty at LSU as an Assistant Professor of Mechanical Engineering.
