Research Projects

Current Projects

06/2015-Present Aerial Robotic Network for Agricultural Applications

Designed and built quadrotor fleet from scratch. Developed autonomous flight control firmware on MCU and flight control/monitor applications on PC. Developed distributed control network for quadrotors. (Funded by the LSU Economic Development Assistantship (EDA) program.)

06/2015-Present Multi-Agent System Formation Control

Developed new formation control methods, provided mathematical proofs, conducted simulational and experimental validation.

Past Projects

10/2015-03/2016 Computational Molecule Synthesis

Developed open-source software which can decompose large molecules to small bio-active fragments, then use fragments to generate target molecules or new molecules for drug design.

02/2015-06/2015 Multi-rotor Copter in Agriculture

Developed an application to process aerial images. Optimized the software for better processing speed.

08/2011-01/2013 Bionic Four-Tail Fin UUV (Mimic Dragonfly)

Developed program on MCU for motor control, developed remote control and wireless data transfer programs on PC and MCU for the bionic UUV with four oscillatory tail fins. The tail fins were designed to mimic the motion pattern of a dragonfly.

11/2011-06/2012 Bionic Long Undulatory Fin UUV (Mimic Black Ghost Knifefish)

Designed and built a UUV to mimic the propulsion pattern of black ghost knifefish. Developed program on Arduino for the UUV. Studied the effects of the frequency and amplitude of the swaying fin ray, wave number in the fin surface, and the speed of the incoming flow on propulsion thrust.

09/2011-05/2012 Bionic Double-Tail Fin UUV

Conducted experiments to optimize the performance of the double tail fin UUV. Developed a whole system to automatically conduct experiment, collect and process data, and plot results.

10/2011-11/2011 Flow Trajectory After Passing Dual Circular Cylinder

Designed and built an adjustable dual circular cylinder model for the fluid experiment. Conducted experiments with hydrogen bubbles, laser beam, and high resolution high speed camera to study the water flow trajectory after passing dual circular cylinder.

04/2011-08/2011 Composite Bionic Actuators

Assisted with motion pattern design. Developed programs on Arduino for the actuators.
